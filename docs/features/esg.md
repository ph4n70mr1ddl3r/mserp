# ESG / Sustainability

Environmental, Social, and Governance tracking integrated into business operations, managed jointly by Report Service (analytics/reporting) and Manufacturing Service (operational data).

| Aspect | Implementation |
|--------|---------------|
| Scope 1 Emissions | Direct emissions from owned/controlled sources (fleet, facilities, manufacturing processes) with automated calculation from activity data |
| Scope 2 Emissions | Indirect emissions from purchased energy (electricity, heat, steam) with grid emission factor integration |
| Scope 3 Emissions | Value chain emissions (suppliers, transportation, product use, end-of-life) with supplier data collection |
| Carbon Accounting | Per-facility, per-product, and per-transaction carbon footprint tracking with allocation methodologies |
| Energy Tracking | Energy consumption monitoring by facility, process, and production run with efficiency trending |
| Water Management | Water consumption tracking with discharge quality monitoring and recycling metrics |
| Waste Management | Waste generation tracking by type (hazardous, recyclable, landfill) with diversion rate calculation |
| ESG Scorecards | Configurable ESG KPIs with targets, trends, peer benchmarking, and traffic-light status |
| Regulatory Reporting | Framework support: GRI, SASB, TCFD, EU Taxonomy, CSRD, ISSB with automated data collection |
| Carbon Offsets | Offset tracking, retirement verification, and registry integration with offset quality scoring |
| Supply Chain Emissions | Supplier carbon footprint data collection with Scope 3 allocation and supplier engagement tracking |
| Product Carbon Footprint | Product-level lifecycle assessment (LCA) with cradle-to-gate and cradle-to-grave calculations |
| ESG Data Governance | ESG data validation rules, audit trail, source documentation, and third-party assurance readiness |
| Decarbonization Planning | Emission reduction target setting with pathway modeling, initiative tracking, and progress monitoring |
| Circular Economy | Material recyclability tracking, take-back program management, and circular design scoring |
| Biodiversity Impact | Land use impact assessment and biodiversity metrics for relevant operations |
| Social Metrics | Diversity & inclusion, labor practices, community impact, and human rights due diligence tracking |

*See [Enterprise Health & Safety](health-safety.md) for workplace safety management. See [Product Compliance](product-compliance.md) for product environmental compliance. See [Architecture Overview](../architecture/overview.md) for system context.*
