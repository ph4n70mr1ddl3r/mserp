# Advanced Enterprise Tax Management

Comprehensive tax determination, calculation, and reporting integrated across all financial transactions, managed by the Finance Service with external tax engine integration.

| Aspect | Implementation |
|--------|---------------|
| Tax Determination | Automated taxability determination based on product classification, jurisdiction, customer exemption status, and transaction type |
| Tax Calculation | Multi-jurisdiction tax calculation with support for VAT, GST, sales tax, use tax, withholding tax, and excise duties |
| Tax Rates & Rules | Configurable tax rate tables with effective dates, tax jurisdiction mapping, and product taxability rules |
| Exemption Management | Customer and product exemption certificate management with validity tracking and renewal alerts |
| Tax Reporting | Jurisdiction-specific tax returns, VAT/GST returns, sales tax reports, and withholding tax certificates |
| Tax Reconciliation | Tax liability reconciliation with general ledger tax accounts, adjustment tracking, and audit trail |
| Vertex / Avalara Integration | Pre-built connector for external tax engines (Vertex, Avalara, Sovos) for complex tax scenarios |
| Tax Lot Tracking | Tax lot assignment for inventory and fixed assets with cost basis and gain/loss calculation |
| Indirect Tax Automation | Automated reverse charge, intra-community supply, and chain transaction tax handling |
| Nexus Management | Multi-state / multi-country nexus tracking with economic nexus thresholds and filing obligation alerts |
| Tax Audit Support | Tax audit trail with transaction-level detail, exemption documentation, and assessment management |
| Withholding Tax | Supplier withholding tax calculation, certificate management, and reporting per jurisdiction |

*See [Multi-Currency](multi-currency.md) for currency handling in tax. See [Revenue Recognition](revenue-recognition.md) for tax in revenue allocation. See [Architecture Overview](../architecture/overview.md) for system context.*
