# Supplier Diversity Management

Comprehensive supplier diversity program management for tracking, reporting, and optimizing diverse supplier participation, managed by the Finance Service (procurement) with Integration Service (certification data).

| Aspect | Implementation |
|--------|---------------|
| Diversity Classification | Supplier diversity classification (minority-owned, women-owned, veteran-owned, small business, HUBZone, LGBTBE, disability-owned) with certification tracking |
| Certification Management | Diversity certification verification, expiration tracking, and automated renewal alerts for third-party certifications (NMSDC, WBENC, SBA, etc.) |
| Spend Tracking | Real-time diverse supplier spend tracking by category, business unit, and diversity classification |
| Tier Reporting | Tier 1 (direct) and Tier 2 (subcontractor) diversity spend reporting with automated roll-up |
| Goal Setting | Configurable diversity spend targets by category, business unit, and period with progress tracking |
| Compliance Reporting | Government-mandated diversity reporting (EEO-1, VETS-4212, OMB reporting) with automated form generation |
| Supplier Development | Diverse supplier development programs with mentoring, capacity building, and progress tracking |
| Sourcing Integration | Diversity criteria in sourcing events with weighted scoring for diverse supplier participation |
| Dashboard & Analytics | Diversity spend dashboards, trend analysis, goal attainment tracking, and benchmark reporting |

*See [Strategic Sourcing & Supplier Risk](sourcing.md) for sourcing events. See [Trade Compliance](trade-compliance.md) for supplier screening. See [Architecture Overview](../architecture/overview.md) for system context.*
