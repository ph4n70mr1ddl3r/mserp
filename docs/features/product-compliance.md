# Product Compliance & Regulatory Management

Product compliance tracking and regulatory management across all markets and jurisdictions, managed by the Manufacturing Service with Integration Service (regulatory data feeds).

| Aspect | Implementation |
|--------|---------------|
| Regulatory Database | Configurable regulatory requirements database by product category, market, and jurisdiction |
| Compliance Tracking | Product compliance status tracking against applicable regulations with gap identification |
| Certification Management | Product certification lifecycle (UL, CE, FCC, FDA, RoHS, REACH, etc.) with renewal tracking |
| Labeling Compliance | Product labeling compliance checks against jurisdiction-specific requirements (GHS, nutritional, textile) |
| Material Compliance | Restricted substance tracking (RoHS, REACH, TSCA, Prop 65) with material declaration management |
| Testing Management | Compliance test plan management with lab integration, result tracking, and certification linkage |
| Market Access | Market-specific compliance requirements for product registration and import/export authorization |
| Regulatory Change Management | Regulatory change monitoring with impact analysis on existing products and compliance gaps |
| Documentation | Automated compliance documentation generation (declaration of conformity, safety data sheets, technical files) |
| Audit Support | Compliance audit trail with documentation packages, evidence collection, and audit finding management |
| Integration | Compliance data feeds into Trade Compliance for export screening and ESG for sustainability reporting |

*See [Trade Compliance](trade-compliance.md) for trade-specific compliance. See [ESG / Sustainability](esg.md) for environmental compliance. See [Architecture Overview](../architecture/overview.md) for system context.*
