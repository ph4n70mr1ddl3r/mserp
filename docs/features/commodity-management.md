# Commodity Management & Trading

Commodity procurement, pricing, and risk management for commodity-dependent businesses, managed by the Finance Service (procurement) with Commerce Service (pricing integration).

| Aspect | Implementation |
|--------|---------------|
| Commodity Catalog | Commodity classification with specifications, grade standards, and market reference pricing |
| Market Price Integration | Real-time commodity market price feeds from exchanges and benchmark providers |
| Contract Management | Commodity purchase contracts with quantity, price, delivery schedules, and escalation clauses |
| Hedging Integration | Commodity hedging position tracking with mark-to-market valuation and hedge effectiveness reporting |
| Price Simulation | What-if commodity price impact analysis on product costs and margins |
| Supply Risk Management | Commodity supply risk assessment with concentration analysis, geographic risk, and alternative sourcing |
| Inventory Valuation | Commodity inventory valuation with market price adjustment and lower-of-cost-or-market rules |
| Futures & Forwards | Forward contract tracking with settlement scheduling and gain/loss recognition |
| Supplier Management | Commodity-specific supplier evaluation with volume allocation and rebate tracking |
| Analytics | Commodity spend analysis, price variance tracking, hedging effectiveness, and forecast accuracy |

*See [Strategic Sourcing & Supplier Risk](sourcing.md) for sourcing events. See [Advanced Pricing Engine](../services/commerce.md) for pricing integration. See [Architecture Overview](../architecture/overview.md) for system context.*
