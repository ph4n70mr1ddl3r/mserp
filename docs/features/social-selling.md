# Embedded Social Selling

Social selling tools integrated into CRM workflows for relationship-building and lead generation, managed by the CRM / Marketing Service.

| Aspect | Implementation |
|--------|---------------|
| Social Profile Enrichment | Automatic enrichment of CRM contacts with social media profile data and activity signals |
| Social Engagement Tracking | Track social interactions (likes, shares, comments, mentions) linked to CRM contacts and opportunities |
| Lead Generation | Social media lead capture with auto-import into CRM lead pipeline with source attribution |
| Social Listening | Monitor brand mentions, competitor activity, and industry conversations with sentiment analysis |
| Content Sharing | Curated content sharing from within CRM with engagement tracking and click analytics |
| Social Intelligence | AI-driven social selling index scoring with recommended actions for sales representatives |
| Relationship Mapping | Social network relationship visualization for identifying warm introduction paths |
| Compliance Guardrails | Configurable social media policy enforcement with content approval workflows for regulated industries |
| Integration | Deep integration with LinkedIn, Twitter/X, and other professional networks via Integration Service connectors |

*See [Customer Data Platform](cdp.md) for unified customer profiles. See [Adaptive Intelligence](adaptive-intelligence.md) for AI-driven insights. See [Architecture Overview](../architecture/overview.md) for system context.*
