# Enterprise Health & Safety (EHS)

Comprehensive health, safety, and environmental management integrated with manufacturing, HR, and ESG reporting, managed by the Manufacturing Service with HR Service integration.

| Aspect | Implementation |
|--------|---------------|
| Incident Management | Safety incident reporting, investigation workflows, root cause analysis, corrective and preventive actions (CAPA) |
| Risk Assessment | Job hazard analysis (JHA), risk matrices, hierarchy of controls, residual risk tracking |
| Permit-to-Work | Digital work permit management with approval workflows, isolation verification, and time-bounded validity |
| Inspection Management | Scheduled and ad-hoc safety inspections with checklists, findings tracking, and corrective action assignment |
| Chemical Management | Safety Data Sheet (SDS) management, hazardous material inventory, exposure tracking, regulatory reporting |
| Occupational Health | Medical surveillance programs, exposure monitoring, health assessments, return-to-work workflows |
| Training & Certification | Safety training requirements tracking, certification expiration alerts, competency verification |
| Near-Miss Reporting | Anonymous near-miss reporting with investigation workflows and trend analysis |
| Emergency Response | Emergency response plans, drill scheduling, evacuation procedures, incident command integration |
| Regulatory Compliance | OSHA, ISO 45001, and jurisdiction-specific regulatory reporting with automated form generation |
| Leading Indicators | Safety observation programs, behavioral safety tracking, proactive risk identification dashboards |
| Integration | Safety data feeds into ESG reporting, HR worker compensation, and manufacturing downtime analysis |

*See [ESG / Sustainability](esg.md) for environmental metrics. See [IoT Integration](iot.md) for sensor-based safety monitoring. See [Architecture Overview](../architecture/overview.md) for system context.*
